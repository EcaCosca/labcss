Apple Pie

We have been trying French onion soup in restaurants for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation.


Ingredients

  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • - 4 cups sliced onions
  • - 4 (10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  • - 1 tablespoons dry sherry (optional)
  • - 4 slices French bread
  • - 4 slices provolone cheese
  • - 2 slices Swiss cheese,
  • - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese


Directions

  • 1- Melt butter with olive oil in an 8 quart stock pot on medium heat. Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  • 2- Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  • 3- Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
  • 4- Heat the oven broiler. Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer). Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly. .
